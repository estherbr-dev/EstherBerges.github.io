Game description

  • Title: Oscar Flying Chicken

  • Genre: Rythm Game
  • Platform: Mobile Android
  • Mode: Single player

Tools used

My responsiblities and missions

  • Programmed the world generation system. The layout is randomized so that the player sees different layouts every time he plays.
  • Programmed the customization system for the chicken and its menus. The player can buy and change the chickens' hats, shirts, shoes, tattoos...
  • Programmed most of the UI and stores. Worked on the hole menu flow from the main menu to in-game UI.
  • Programmed social and sharing features. The player can take pictures of himself with his chicken and post them to Facebook.
  • Designed different rhythm patterns that would appear in-game and game balancing.
