Atomic Days

Game Description

Atomic days is a board game where you also use your phone. In this game, four players are leading four countries that are divided into two factions. Each player controls several cards that they will try to exchange with their partner each turn. When a faction tries to pass a card to his ally, the opposition will try to steal it and if a player acquires enough cards his faction wins the game. While the players play, an omniscient entity will monitor their movements, reveal secret information and sanction/help each faction as it pleases.

My responsiblities and missions

  • Programmed the mobile phone app: created all the game menus and the AI behavior that would help or hinder the players.
  • Created the code bar lector that allows the phone to know which cards the player tries to give to his ally.
  • Designed and programmed the app to help the player keep the game score and tell them what they need to do each turn.
