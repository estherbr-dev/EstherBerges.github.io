Latest project

FARCRY 6

Senior Technical Level Designer at Ubisoft Berlin

I am currently working on FARCRY 6! This is a AAA open-world game where the player will have to fight against an oppressive government to liberate his country from the dictator Anton Castillo.
On a day-to-day basis I mainly focus on supporting content creators to implement, debug and validate features they will create in engine. Most of my work consists on:

  • Create, debug and teach visual scripts.
  • Setting up databases to create or derivate features like AI behaviors, loot, gameplay ingredients...
  • Work with the design team to create gameplay features from prototypes to completion.
  • Validate the gameplay content created in the editor by LDs, GDs or NDs.
  • Prepare pieces of training for the content creators.
  • Manage other technical level designers.(tr
  • Investigate and improve our internal processes.
